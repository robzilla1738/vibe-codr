# Code of conduct

Be decent to each other. Concretely:

- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- No harassment, slurs, or personal attacks — anywhere in the project's
  spaces, including issues, PRs, and discussions.
- Assume good faith, especially with first-time contributors and people
  writing in a second language.
- Disagreements are fine; grinding an argument on after a maintainer has made
  a call is not.

If someone's behavior is a problem, email robertcourson96@gmail.com. Reports
are read by the maintainer and kept private. Consequences scale with the
behavior, from a warning to a permanent ban.
